How a microwave oven works

A microwave oven heats food using electromagnetic waves, which are called microwaves. These waves make water molecules in the food move quickly, creating heat. This process cooks food evenly and quickly. The first microwave oven was made in the 1940s. Over time, they became smaller and cheaper for homes. Today, microwaves are used to heat leftovers and cook many types of food. They are very popular in kitchens worldwide.

How does a credit card work?

A credit card is a payment card issued by a bank. It allows people to buy things or take out cash on credit. This means the user can spend money they do not have right away, but they must pay it back later. Credit cards can build a balance that may have interest charged. Unlike charge cards, which require payment in full each month, credit cards let users carry a balance. They are different from debit cards, which use money directly from a bank account.
