How does inflation work?

Inflation is when prices for goods and services rise. This means that money buys less than before. We measure inflation with a number called the consumer price index (CPI). When inflation goes up, people may spend less because they worry about prices rising more. Some reasons for inflation include changes in how much people want to buy and changes in supply. While inflation can cause problems, like making it hard to plan for the future, it can also help the economy by lowering unemployment. Most experts agree that a small, steady amount of inflation is best for the economy. ...
